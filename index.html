Tips for Transporting TVs

  • Always transport TVs upright when possible to prevent screen damage
  • Use blankets or padding to protect the TV during transport
  • Removing the TV from the box will forfeit the right to return it.
  • Consider folding rear seats down for extra cargo space
  • For larger TVs, consider delivery or rental van services
  • Check your car's maximum cargo opening dimensions in the manual
